The Evolution

Founded in 2026 by Skitxoe, EchoDupeLS represents the next generation of competitive Minecraft.

Our roots trace back to the Minehut community, where we were originally known as VoidDupes. While the name has changed, our core philosophy remains: providing a high-octane environment where strategy and skill dictate your survival.

We have evolved from a simple dupe server into a complex Lifesteal experience. Every heart you take is a step toward dominance; every heart you lose brings you closer to the void.
