GLOBALRULES

Official Server Regulations & Conduct

Minecraft Section

No Hacking

Using hacked clients, X-ray packs, or any unfair mods is a permanent ban.

No Spawn Griefing

Do not break blocks, place lava/water, or ruin the community spawn area.

No Spawn Killing

Do not kill players at the world spawn or at their set home/bed locations.

No Alt-Account Killing

Do not use secondary accounts to farm kills or inflate your stats.

No Slurs

Zero tolerance for racial, homophobic, or hateful slurs in the game chat.

No Combat Logging

Do not disconnect during a fight to save your items.

No Lag Machines

Do not build redstone clocks or entity pits designed to slow the server.

No Exploiting

Do not use dupe glitches or game-breaking bugs.

No Ban Evasion

If you are banned, do not log in on an alt to bypass it.

Respect Staff

If a moderator tells you to stop a behavior, follow their instructions.

Notice: Violation of these rules will result in an immediate ban.

Discord Section

No Slurs or Hate Speech

Any form of hate speech or harassment results in an immediate ban.

No Doxxing

Sharing or threatening to share real-life information is prohibited.

No Excessive Spam

Do not flood channels with repetitive messages or wall of text.

Keep it Organized

Use the correct channels for their intended purpose.

No Unauthorized Advertising

Do not DM members or post links to other servers without permission.

SFW Content Only

No NSFW images, links, or suggestive profile pictures/nicknames.

No Malicious Links

Do not post IP loggers, grabbers, or any suspicious files.

No Ghost Pinging

Do not ping users and then delete the message.

Respect Privacy

Do not record voice chats or share private DMs without consent.

Account Security

You are responsible for your account; excuses will not be accepted.

Notice: Violation of these rules will result in an immediate ban.
