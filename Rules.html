Protocol Rules

Server Conduct

Ignorance of rules is not an excuse for a ban.

01

Fair Play & Cheating

The use of hacked clients, unfair scripts, or automated macros that provide a competitive advantage is strictly prohibited. This is a dupe server, but play fair with your movements.

02

Communication & Respect

Toxic behavior, excessive spam, or any form of discrimination will result in an immediate mute or ban. Keep the banter competitive but clean.

03

Exploitation

While duplication is the heart of the server, exploiting server-breaking bugs that crash the network or lag out other players is a permanent ban offense.

04

Staff Authority

Staff decisions are final. If you feel a punishment was unjust, use the proper channels on the Discord to file an appeal rather than arguing in-game.
