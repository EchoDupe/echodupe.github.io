Network Statutes

In-Game Directives

Global Gameplay Restrictions

Integrity & Fair Play

Utilization of hacked clients, X-ray packs, or prohibited modifications will result in a permanent suspension.

Spawn Jurisdiction

Destruction of infrastructure or liquid placement within the community spawn perimeter is strictly forbidden.

Engagement Ethics

Targeting players at world spawn or designated respawn points (Beds/Homes) is prohibited.

Stat Manipulation

The use of secondary accounts to artificially inflate kill counts or network statistics is disallowed.

Communication Standards

Zero tolerance for racial, homophobic, or derogatory slurs within public or private channels.

Combat Continuity

Disconnecting during active engagement to circumvent item loss (Combat Logging) is an enforceable offense.

Technical Stability

Construction of redstone clocks or entity-dense structures designed to degrade server performance is prohibited.

Exploitation Policy

intentional use of game-breaking bugs or unauthorized duplication glitches is strictly forbidden.

Sanction Evasion

Attempting to bypass active bans via alternative accounts will result in a permanent network blacklisting.

Staff Authority

Official directives issued by the Administrative team must be followed without exception.

Notice: Failure to comply with these statutes will result in immediate disciplinary action, including permanent account suspension.

Community Conduct

Digital Communication Guidelines

Harassment Policy

Any form of hate speech or targeted harassment will result in an immediate and final removal.

Privacy Protection

The distribution or threat of sharing PII (Personally Identifiable Information) is strictly prohibited.

Channel Discipline

Avoid excessive spamming, emoji flooding, or repetitive messaging within community channels.

Content Organization

Ensure all media and dialogue are localized to their designated thematic channels.

Solicitation Policy

Unauthorized advertising via DMs or public links is prohibited without prior Admin approval.

Media Standards

Only SFW content is permitted. This includes avatars, nicknames, and shared media.

Cyber Security

Distribution of malicious software, IP loggers, or suspicious URLs is a blacklisting offense.

Engagement Protocol

Ghost pinging (tagging and deleting) is considered disruptive behavior and is subject to mute.

Confidentiality

Unauthorized recording of voice channels or leaking private correspondences is forbidden.

Account Liability

Users are solely responsible for all actions taken on their account. Negligence is not an excuse.

Notice: Violations of this conduct agreement may lead to permanent removal from the Discord network.
