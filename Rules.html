ECHODUPE LAWS

In-Game Protocol

Minecraft Server Rules

No Hacking

Hacked clients, X-ray, or unfair mods result in a permanent ban.

No Spawn Griefing

Do not break blocks or ruin the community spawn area.

No Spawn Killing

Do not kill players at spawn or their set home/bed locations.

No Alt-Account Killing

Do not use secondary accounts to farm kills or stats.

No Slurs

Zero tolerance for racial, homophobic, or hateful slurs.

No Combat Logging

Do not disconnect during a fight to save your items.

No Lag Machines

No redstone clocks or entity pits designed to slow the server.

No Exploiting

Do not use dupe glitches or game-breaking bugs.

No Ban Evasion

Do not use alt accounts to bypass a ban.

Respect Staff

Follow moderator instructions immediately.

Discord Conduct

Community Server Rules

No Slurs or Hate Speech

Harassment results in an immediate and permanent ban.

No Doxxing

Sharing real-life info is strictly prohibited.

No Excessive Spam

No flooding channels with messages, emojis, or walls of text.

Keep it Organized

Use the correct channels for their intended purpose.

No Unauthorized Ads

Do not DM members or post links to other servers.

SFW Content Only

No NSFW images, links, or suggestive profiles.

No Malicious Links

No IP loggers, grabbers, or suspicious files.

No Ghost Pinging

Do not ping users and then delete the message.

Respect Privacy

No recording voice chats or sharing private DMs.

Account Security

You are responsible for your account at all times.
